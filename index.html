Why This Method Works for Every Knowledge Worker

Every knowledge worker follows a similar decision loop, whether they work in sales, engineering, finance, operations, or strategy.

Example: Sales performance analysis through the same 5-step method.

Step 1: Set Objective

Define the business question, scope, and success criteria.

Step 2: Collect Inputs

Gather relevant data and context into one clean evidence pack.

Step 3: Analyze Insights

Compare trends and variance to identify root causes.

Step 4: Deliver Output
